Office ACVM Fit-Out

We are well-versed in designing and building for the air-conditioning and mechanical ventilation systems of offices. A well-ventilated, comfortable office is after all crucial for a conducive working environment. We have executed projects in commercial buildings such as Mapletree Business City and Marina Bay Financial Centre.

F&B ACVM Fit-Out

We have designed and installed AC and mechanical ventilation systems for a wide range of F&B businesses of varying sizes and complexities, from cafes to teppanyaki restaurants to buffets. Good ACVM ensures that there is no smoke, odor or excessive heat where your customers are enjoying their dining experience. Our completed projects include prominent F&B businesses such as P.S. Cafe and Sakae Sushi.

F&B Kitchen Hood and Exhaust

We also provide installation and repair of kitchen hood and exhaust for F&B businesses. Kitchen hoods and exhausts of appropriate airflow and quality go hand in hand with the ACVM of your F&B business, in ensuring an odor-free and comfortable dining experience for your customers. Our completed projects include well-known clients such as Tung Lok and Thai Express.

Laboratory ACVM Fit-Out

Designing and outfitting the ACMV for laboratories are usually complex projects. However, we are experienced in designing and building for the strict regulations and requirements of laboratories. Our completed projects include established labs such as ADM Asia and Ingredion Singapore.

Frequently Asked Question

You May Ask

I am not an interior designer. Can I still hire Win Tat for its services?
While the majority of our work is done with interior designers, we certainly have years of experience working with end users (e.g. F&B business owners). Feel free to give us a call today and discuss your needs.
What is the approximate time needed for a project to be completed?
This is highly variable depending on the complexity and size of the project, including the structure of the building that the business is situated in. For the average project of medium complexity (e.g. the average restaurant), it takes 6-8 weeks for design and installation. Do give us a call to get a price estimate and time estimate for your project.
Does Win Tat do home installation and repairs?
We are currently providing services for only commercial businesses and do not offer home installation/repairs.
I have already hired a consultant to do ACVM design. Can I hire Win Tat for the installation works?
Yes, we work well with consultants and will offer value-added designs upon request. Do give us a call to discuss your needs and our services.
