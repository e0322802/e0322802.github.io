Win Tat Air-Conditioning Pte Ltd

40 Years Experience

Are you looking to outfit your office, restaurant or laboratory with AC and ventilation systems? Are you an interior designer looking for a contractor of such needs for your client? If so, you've come to the right place. At Win Tat Air-Conditioning Pte Ltd, we are well-versed in projects for commercial office fit-out, F&B fit-out, and laboratories. Our services also include kitchen hood and kitchen exhaust installation/repair.

Win Tat Air Conditioning Pte Ltd is a BCA Registered Company Under Workhead MEOI. With decades of experience under our belt, you can be assured of timely and quality work for competitive prices. We have worked with designers hired by prominent companies such as Govtech Singapore, Bank of China, Marina Bay Sands, Sakae Sushi and ADM Asia.
