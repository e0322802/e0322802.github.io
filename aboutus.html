Our Services

BCA Registered

Win Tat Air Conditioning Pte Ltd is a BCA Registered Company Under Workhead MEOI, incorporated since 1981.

Leave your needs in the hands of our capable and experienced team. We have a full complement of project personnnel, from a project director to in-house workers. With decades of experience under our belt, you can be assured of timely and quality work for competitive prices.

Design & Build

We specialise in 'Design & Build' projects, in which we design the AC system based on clients' requirements, followed by installation works, and finish with a satisfactory handover to clients. We have worked with multiple designers hired by prominent companies such as Govtech Singapore, Bank of China, Marina Bay Sands, Sakae Sushi and ADM Asia.

Frequently Asked Question

You May Ask

I am not an interior designer. Can I still hire Win Tat for its services?
While the majority of our work is done with interior designers, we certainly have years of experience working with Direct Clients (e.g. F&B business owners). Feel free to give us a call today and discuss your needs.
What is the approximate time needed for a project to be completed?
This is highly variable depending on the complexity and size of the project, including the structure of the building that the business is situated in. For the average project of medium complexity (e.g. the average restaurant), it takes 6-8 weeks for design and installation. Do give us a call to get a price estimate and time estimate for your project.
Does Win Tat do home installation and repairs?
We are currently providing services for only commercial businesses and do not offer home installation/repairs.
I have already hired a consultant to do ACVM design. Can I hire Win Tat for the installation works?
This depends on the design produced by the consultant. However, we have certainly worked with consultants in the past, chiefly in making further improvements to the design and then completing the installation works. Do give us a call to discuss your needs and our services.
